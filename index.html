How it works

Three swipes to a clean gallery

🗑

Swipe Left

Mark photos and videos for deletion. They go to a review list first.

❤

Swipe Right

Keep what you love. These files won't appear in future sessions.

✅

Review & Delete

Check your selection, then delete everything at once. You stay in control.

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

Album Filter

Clean specific albums one at a time or shuffle everything together.

Photo & Video Modes

Focus on just photos or just videos — your choice.

Full Preview

Tap to view any file in full screen before deciding.

Video Playback

Auto-plays videos with sound control and seek bar.

Undo Anytime

Changed your mind? One tap brings a file back.

Storage Stats

Track deleted files and freed space across all sessions.

Your photos stay yours

PicSwipe works 100% offline. No accounts, no cloud uploads, no tracking. Files are deleted through your device's system dialog — you always confirm first.
